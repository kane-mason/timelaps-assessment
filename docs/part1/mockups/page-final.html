The bottom line · where to focus

Aurora is steady, but squeezed top and bottom.

It holds a comfortable #2 — well clear of Cascade and Drift — but Borealis is winning new awareness fast (the gap there widened most), and converts far more of its considerers into buyers. Aurora's bright spot is consideration: it's growing and the only gap that's closing.

Where to focus → Defend awareness while Borealis surges, and turn Aurora's rising consideration into buyers — that's where the funnel leaks most.
