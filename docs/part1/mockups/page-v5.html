The takeaway
What a marketing leader should do about it
01

Aurora is a stable #2 and as liked as the leader — clear of Cascade and Drift, with demand as loyal as Borealis's.

02

The consideration gap is mostly reach: once people know both brands, Aurora nearly matches Borealis — so buy awareness.

03

The fixable weakness is trial, sharpest among women — and it's worth roughly +3.8pp of usage.
