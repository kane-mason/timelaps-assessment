Why the gap diverges · and where to focus

Gaining in the middle, losing at the edges.

Aurora is a comfortable #2 — clear of Cascade and Drift. Its consideration gain is a genuine conversion win: more of the people who know Aurora now consider it. But that interest isn't reaching the till — Aurora turns considerers into buyers at ~48% while Borealis jumped to ~60%, and Borealis is also winning new awareness fast. So the gap closes in the middle and widens at both ends.

Where to focus → Fix the lower-funnel leak — turn rising consideration into trial — while defending awareness against Borealis's surge.
